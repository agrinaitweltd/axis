Coffee

Premium Arabica & Robusta from the highlands of East Africa

Our Coffee

East African Coffee Excellence

East Africa produces some of the most sought-after coffee in the world. Axis International sources and exports premium grade Arabica and Robusta coffee beans from Uganda, Kenya, and Tanzania delivering to roasters, commodity traders, and importers worldwide.

We supply both green (unroasted) and processed coffee in a range of grades and screen sizes, tailored to buyer specifications. Whether you need a single origin lot or a consistent commercial blend grade, we can deliver.

Varieties

What We Supply

Arabica Coffee

High altitude, washed and natural process Arabica from Mt. Elgon (Uganda), Nyeri & Kirinyaga (Kenya), and Kilimanjaro & Mbeya (Tanzania). Known for bright acidity, complex flavour, and excellent cup scores.

Robusta Coffee

Uganda is the world's second-largest Robusta producer. We supply Screen 15+ and Screen 18 FAQ grades ideal for espresso blends, instant coffee, and commercial use. Consistent, high-volume supply.

Specialty & Single Origin

For specialty roasters, we can source single-origin micro-lots with full traceability, cupping notes, and certifications including Organic, Rainforest Alliance, and Fair Trade where available.

Standards

Quality & Export Details

Grading & Sorting

All coffee is graded by screen size, moisture content, defect count, and cup quality. Sorted electronically and by hand to meet export standards.

Packaging

Standard export packaging in GrainPro-lined 60kg jute bags. Custom packaging available for specialty and branded orders.

Certifications

We can supply certified Organic, Fair Trade, Rainforest Alliance, and UTZ coffee depending on origin and buyer requirements.

Shipping

FCL (full container load) sea freight from Mombasa or Dar es Salaam. Sample shipments and LCL available. Air freight for urgent or small-volume specialty lots.

The Advantage

Why Source Coffee From East Africa?

Ideal Growing Conditions

High altitude, volcanic soils, and equatorial climate produce coffee with exceptional flavour complexity and body.

Origin Traceability

Every lot can be traced to its source farm or cooperative, giving roasters and buyers full provenance details.

Competitive Pricing

Direct farm-gate procurement and lower intermediary costs mean competitive pricing without compromising quality.
