Avocado Supply

Reliable Export Programmes for Wholesale Buyers

Axis Agro International Limited sources export-grade avocados through trusted farms and pack-houses in East Africa. We support buyers looking for consistent Hass and Fuerte supply, clear product specifications, and practical logistics from origin to destination.

Our team coordinates grading, packing, cold chain handling, phytosanitary documentation, and shipment planning for importers, distributors, food service suppliers, and fresh produce wholesalers.
