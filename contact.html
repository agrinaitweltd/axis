Common Questions

Before You Reach Out

What is the minimum order quantity?

Minimum orders vary by product. We accommodate both FCL (full container) and smaller LCL orders. Contact us with your requirements for a tailored quote.

Do you export outside the UK?

Yes. We supply international buyers worldwide. Our export capability covers Europe, the Middle East, Asia, and the Americas. Contact us to discuss your destination.

Can we request product samples?

Yes. Pre-shipment samples and cupping reports (for coffee) are available on request. Please mention this in your enquiry and we will arrange accordingly.

What documentation do you provide?

We provide full export documentation including phytosanitary certificates, certificates of origin, commercial invoices, packing lists, and fumigation reports where required.
