Our Company

Who We Are

Axis International Limited is a UK-registered agricultural trading company with a clear purpose: to bridge the gap between East Africa's exceptional agricultural produce and the global market demand for quality food products.

We specialise in the sourcing, import, and export of agricultural commodities including coffee, fresh fruits, grains, and other farm produce. We supply wholesale to companies across the United Kingdom and export to international buyers around the world.

At the heart of our operation is a dedicated team of specialists based in East Africa primarily Uganda who work directly with farmers and cooperatives to ensure every product we trade meets the highest standards of quality and traceability.

Purpose & Direction

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide UK and international businesses with a reliable, high-quality supply of premium agricultural produce sourced ethically from East Africa while creating positive economic impact for the farming communities we work with.

Our Vision

To become a leading name in UK agricultural trade, recognised for our integrity, the quality of our produce, and the strength of our partnerships from the farms of Uganda to the wholesale markets of the world.

Our Journey

How We Got Here

Founded in the UK

Axis International Limited was established in the United Kingdom with a founding mission to create a premium agricultural trading operation built on trust, quality, and genuine field expertise.

East Africa Operations Established

We deployed a specialist team on the ground in Uganda and East Africa, building direct relationships with growers, cooperatives and exporters to ensure quality control at source.

UK Wholesale Supply Launched

We began supplying premium agricultural produce to wholesale buyers across the United Kingdom, with a growing portfolio covering coffee, fresh fruits, grains and more.

International Export Growth

Our trade network expanded to serve international buyers, with export capabilities covering multiple continents and diverse product categories.

Today Building a Sustainable Future

We continue to grow our network, deepen our sourcing expertise, and expand our product range all while maintaining our commitment to quality, ethics, and partnership.
