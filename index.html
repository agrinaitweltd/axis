Who We Are

Connecting East Africa's Finest to the World

Axis Agro International Limited is a UK and Uganda-registered agricultural trading company specialising in the import and export of high-quality produce. We supply wholesale to businesses across the UK and export to international markets.

Our dedicated team of specialists on the ground in East Africa particularly Uganda works directly with farmers to source produce of the highest quality, ensuring traceability and ethical standards at every step.
